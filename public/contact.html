FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does Urban Wash Club work?
It's simple. You get a one month trial and we wash your street facing windows on groung and first floor for just £1. After your trial month, we’ll continue coming by and wash your windows for £20 per month.
What if I don't need my windows washed that often?
No problem. Once you join the Club, you can change frequency to every other month. You can also skip a month anytime you want or pause subscription for up to three months.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel anytime, for any reason with just a few clicks. We hope you'll stay, but we don't make it hard to leave.
What if I have a flat?
No problem. You can specify on which floor is your flat when you sign up. Your monthly subscription will be £11.
How will I know when will Urban Washers come?
We will send you email one day before and let you know what time you should expect them. No need to meet them as they will work from the street. They will leave a card in your mail to inform you about their visit.
